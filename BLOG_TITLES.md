## Recommendation

I recommend **Option 1** (Combined Blog) because:
1. It tells a complete story
2. The AI comparison is naturally woven into the narrative
3. It's more engaging than separate posts
4. Readers get both the product story and the tool comparison
5. It's unique - not many people have compared these tools side-by-side

The title "Building RecordPanel: A Screen Recording SDK Powered by AI Pair Programming" works well because:
- It's clear what the product is
- It highlights the unique AI aspect
- It's SEO-friendly
- It's intriguing without being clickbait
